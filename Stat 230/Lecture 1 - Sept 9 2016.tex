\subsection{What does probability mean?}

\begin{itemize}
\item The term \textbf{probability} refers to the study of randomness (variability of results).

\item Probability is used to quantify the likelihood, or chance, that an outcome of random experiment will occur.

 \item By variability, we mean successive observations of a system or phenomenon do \textbf{not} produce exactly the same result.
\end{itemize}
